** What do you do for fun? **

Superfun Games is a family game shop in Austin, TX. Founded by Clark and Angie Wimberly (and loved by all), we make clever, quick card games and puzzles that prioritize group play over complex rules.

Yes, Chef! is our first release, the cooking card game where you fight to collect ingredients and match recipes in an epic chef battle.

Innovation is at the heart of what we do. Our puzzle games are created to push the boundaries of conventional thinking and stimulate your problem-solving skills. Each puzzle is a work of art, intricately designed to offer a rewarding and immersive experience. Whether you're deciphering cryptic clues, navigating complex mazes, or piecing together beautiful mosaics, our puzzles promise hours of engaging fun. We continually explore new ideas and concepts to keep our offerings fresh and exciting.
