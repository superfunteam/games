In The Grass

A really dumb (and not at all finished) pulp game for the Playdate handheld console. Built in airports during delays.

* Actual Gameplay Footage *

In the Grass is a little game I made while stuck in an airport. Then on the plane. Then at home a little.

Oh and it doesn’t have an ending. Yet.

This was made in Pulp, the online editor. It’s definitely inspired by Eyeland, another game made in Pulp. If you like games, please go buy that one.
